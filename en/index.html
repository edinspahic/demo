Team

Who we are:

Our interdisciplinary team of architects, civil engineers and software developers specializes in BIM-specific planning services and digitization within BIM standards as well as the development of parametric content and individual software solutions for construction companies. Our core business is the preparation of the implementation planning (LPH 5) and the associated project support during construction.
With years of experience, we now implement BIM projects on an international level, from Switzerland, Germany to Norway. With our knowledge we support planning offices and general contractors in the implementation of BIM projects.
The technical competence of our architects and civil engineers together with our IT development department enables us to implement solutions faster and more efficiently and to be constantly up to date in the digital planning process. Striving for innovative digital solutions shapes our development.

Competencies

BIM Modelling

Model based planning

We create BIM models based on existing planning documents or digitize your existing plans within BIM standards. We develop the building as an intelligent digital 3D model with all relevant data from which all project documentation can be created.

We also support our customers during the planning phase with the creation of BIM models in the necessary degree of detail according to customer requirements.

Content Development

As an official Autodesk development partner, we create parametric content in Revit (Revit Families) from scratch or adapt existing ones. We maintain the model with the exact data and model content throughout the entire course of the project, so that all the advantages of BIM are used as added value.

Creation and Vectorization of Point Cloud

We also create 3D models of buildings that have already been built. With our service, we support you with digitization and we create a 3D building model for you from your 3D point clouds, as well as 2D CAD data as a floor plan, section and view.

BIM Management

BIM Coordination

In a central model created by us, we organize and coordinate all the data from the specialist planners and can thus check whether all the details in the construction work together. We use the BIM360 platform to create a coordination room, which means that uploaded models are coordinated and automatically checked for collisions. In this way, we can eliminate defects in advance and solve problems that may arise on the construction site in good time.

BEP - BIM Execution Plan

The BEP sets the course for everyone and is intended to make the planning process safer, reduce the error rate and increase quality. At the beginning of every construction project, we create an individual process development plan. It contains all of the client's specifications and provides a clear project structure. It is binding for everyone involved in the planning process and is constantly being developed further in the course of the planning process.

BIM Quality Management

The quality of the 3D model is the most important element of the BIM method and the basis for all further evaluations and results. Therefore, an important point of our services is the examination of the planning model with regard to quality and its information content. By checking regulations, errors and defects in BIM models are recognized and communicated on an interdisciplinary basis, and compliance with building regulations and planning specifications is ensured.

Innovation and Development

Software Development

In customer-specific software development, the process of developing solutions is tailored to your specific organizational requirements. It stands out from the standard software because it offers exactly the functions that you need to improve and optimize your current work requirements. The software is developed with your company's implementation needs in mind, so it will only work for your company. A custom program can easily grow with your business, expand to meet new needs, and adapt to changes.

We specialize in developing custom digital solutions (add-ins for Autodesk and standalone apps, web apps, etc.) for construction companies.

Process Optimization

Agile projects and agile work in general put flexibility and adaptability during the project at the top of the list.

In contrast to a waterfall-based approach, in which the ability to plan and fixed framework conditions significantly determine the course of the project, agile projects are able to adapt to changes quickly avoiding unnecessary bureaucracy - both with regard to the product that is being developed and with regard to the team, its methodologies and set-up.

On the basis of existing methods, we develop individual digital processes in which we optimize process flows together with our customers using the BIM model and thus achieve added value for the project.

Cooperation from different locations

Through our experience of working with clients from all over the world, we have developed a strong remote working culture that enables us to meet your needs regardless of location. Strong collaboration and conscious lateral thinking enable us to develop unique solutions for our customers.
